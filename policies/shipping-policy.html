Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2025

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. Estimated delivery time is 3–7 business days.

Delivery Delays

Delivery delays can occasionally occur due to high demand or courier issues. We’ll notify you via email/SMS in such cases.

Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking

You will receive a shipment confirmation email with tracking number once your order has been shipped.

Shipping Address

Please make sure your shipping address is correct. We are not responsible for delays or losses due to incorrect address information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about your order or shipping, please contact us at support@googlemobileshop.com.

Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2025

Returns

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. If 7 days have passed since your purchase, unfortunately we cannot offer you a refund or exchange.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Product must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Product must be in the original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order ID) is required.

Non-returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable, such as opened software, downloaded digital goods, and gift cards.

Refunds (if applicable)

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you about the status of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days back to your original method of payment.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your bank or credit card company. If you still have not received your refund, please contact us at support@googlemobileshop.com.

Exchanges (if applicable)

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, contact us with your order details.

Shipping Returns

To return your product, mail it to: Google Mobile Shop, Returns Department, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding returns and refunds, please contact us at support@googlemobileshop.com.
